3rd Place — Case Competition

Dell Tech Academy

Supply Chain · Dell Technologies · 2025

The Dell Tech Academy case competition was a two-day intensive hosted by Dell Technologies focused on real supply-chain optimization problems faced by enterprise hardware manufacturing.

I worked with Isaac Torres, Aden Ramirez, and Luis Cruz Garfio to break down the brief, model the constraints, and present a recommendation. Our analysis covered inventory positioning, multi-tier supplier risk, and cycle-time tradeoffs — and the final pitch held up under cross-examination from Dell's panel.

We placed 3rd overall, which reflected both the strength of the cohort and the hands-on industry exposure the program is designed to give engineering students stepping into supply-chain-adjacent roles.
