1st Place — $1,000 Award

Pioneers 21 — Tacos & Tech-ila

Holiday Mixer · Microsoft & Nusenda · December 2025

Pioneers 21's holiday mixer brought together the strongest emerging ventures across the El Paso – Las Cruces corridor, hosted by Microsoft and Nusenda Credit Union.

Pitching The Sense Cube alongside ventures including Wealthy, NG Leg Bot, Signology LLC, and BrightSteps, the project closed the night in 1st place — a $1,000 award and an invitation to continue conversations with the host program.

The pitch focused on what makes The Sense Cube unique in the accessibility hardware space: a self-contained Braille translation device that doesn't depend on a phone in the user's hand, designed to be carried like a thick deck of cards, with messaging arriving as physical pin patterns the user can read by touch.
