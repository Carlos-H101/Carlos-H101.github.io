2nd Place — Bilingual Pitch

Miner Tank Pitch Competition

Texas Western Hall · UTEP · 2025

Miner Tank is UTEP's flagship Shark-Tank-style competition. I pitched The Sense Cube in front of a panel of investors, faculty, and industry mentors — and made it the first bilingual pitch in the event's history.

When a judge asked a question whose answer mattered most to the people the device is for — Spanish-speaking communities along the U.S./Mexico border — I answered the rest of the pitch in Spanish. It let me convey the social impact of accessible Braille hardware without flattening it through translation, and it framed the product the way it should be framed: built for the people who need it.

The judges awarded the project 2nd place overall and called out the bilingual delivery as a defining moment of the night.
